Selection Process

  1. Documentary Screening
    We carefully go through each application and offer selected applicants an interview. It may take one to two weeks to notify the result.

  2. First Interview
    First interview is only offered to applicants selected.

  3. Second Interview
    Second interview is done by the department director. We may have more interviews if necessary.

  4. Job Offering
    We send you an affer letter to those who are selected in the final stage where we evaluate all the materials and interviews.

  5. Joining Us
    We discuss when you can join us.

Application

You can apply for the jobs at ngi vietnam through the contact form. Please review the job description and send us necessary information. You may be asked to submit your resume or CV in the reply from our personnel.

Please do not forget to give us the followings:

  • Your name
  • Which job you would like to apply for
  • Why you would like to work for us
  • How you can help us if you are hired
  • When you can join us
