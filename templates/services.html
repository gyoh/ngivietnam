Our Services

ngi vietnam provides various management and consulting services in the field of investment, business incubation and software development. We also dispatch, delegate and coordinate agile offshore software development in Vietnam.

Investment Management and Consulting

ngi vietnam provides consulting and support services of the Vietnam venture fund owned by ngi group, our parent company in Japan. We also provide EIR (entrepreneur in residence) whose role is to spend time with a portfolio company to provide his or her functional expertise. The EIR will sometimes enter the company as a full time executive (typically CEO or some “C” level role) if the company and the executive feel there is a good fit. Finally, we provide monitoring services of portfolio companies to keep track of their business and financial status.

Business Incubation Management and Consulting

ngi vietnam helps Vietnamese local entrepreneurs to start up a new business in Vietnam and provides them with various consulting services to share incubation know-hows accumulated in the ngi group since 1998. We also provide them with the software development management service that lets them outsource their software development to other local companies and helps them manage the development process.

Offshore Development Management and Consulting

ngi vietnam supports companies in Japanese IT industry to develop their software through our offshore development network. We first act as a matchmaker for Japanese companies who are seeking their partners in Vietnam to outsource software development and Vietnamese software engineering companies who are trying to find their customers in Japan. After the matchmaking is successful, we then become the bridge between them and provides them with various project management services through our unique agile offshore development methodology.
